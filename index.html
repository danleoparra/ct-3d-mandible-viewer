Interactive 3D CT Reconstruction of an Equine Mandibular Lesion

Massive mandibular osteolysis associated with ameloblastic carcinoma in a horse: clinical, computed tomographic, necropsy, histopathological, and immunohistochemical findings

Three-dimensional reconstruction generated from computed tomography segmentation to support spatial assessment of the affected mandibular region.

Use mouse or touchscreen gestures to rotate, zoom, and inspect the model. This interactive reconstruction is intended to complement the tomographic images and facilitate three-dimensional anatomical interpretation.
3D model derived from CT segmentation. Provided for scientific visualization only.
